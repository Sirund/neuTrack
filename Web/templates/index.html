Frequently Asked Questions

Uncover insights into NeuTrack's user-friendly features and enhance your experience.

NeuTrack is a smart glove designed to enhance the independence of visually impaired individuals and Alzheimer. It uses advanced technologies like Text-to-Speech, Speech-to-Text, and object recognition to provide real-time information about their surroundings, assist in navigation, and identify objects.
While NeuTrack is designed with features catering to visual impairments and Alzheimer, its versatility makes it useful for a broader audience. The device's functionalities, such as Text-to-Speech and Speech-to-Text, can benefit a range of users.
The A* Algorithm is employed for efficient pathfinding and navigation. It optimizes the route based on real-time GPS data, ensuring the wearer is guided along the safest and most accessible paths, particularly beneficial for individuals with visual impairments and Alzheimer.
